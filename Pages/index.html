About QuickFit

QuickFit is a virtual fitting room integratable with online clothing stores. We provide a solution for these who can`t find their size when shopping online.

Our goal is to:

  • Improve the online shoping experience.
  • Help customers to find clothes that fit well.
  • Reduce waste of returned clothes.
